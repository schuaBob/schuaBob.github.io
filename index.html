Advanced Real Time Game Statistic

Problem Identification

In strategic video games like card games or chess, although tools exist to calculate the optimal win rate for the next round based on a chosen action, experienced players often opt for actions they subjectively deem the best, challenging the predictions of rational choice models.

Design Implication

The proposed solution is the development of an "Advanced Real Time Game Statistic AI" system to integrate ToM into video card games. Utilizing game theory and multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms, the system provides advanced feedback beyond win rates. It challenges players' expectations, offers insights into optimal actions, and encourages self-reflection by justifying non-promoted moves.

Evaluation Plan

This seciton involves professional and amateur players playing the game twice: once following the highest win rate action and once following system-promoted actions aligned with their beliefs. The win rates of these approaches will be compared to assess the system's effectiveness. The study aims to understand the impact of consistent experience in complex real-time multiplayer video card games, but the expected results lack a clear hypothesis and consideration of potential limitations.
